  • rdfs:comment "In France, a hospital practitioner (PH) is a doctor, surgeon, pharmacist, or dentist practicing in a public health institution or a residential care facility for dependent elderly people (EHPAD). In the strict sense, only those who, recruited at the end of an annual national competition, are permanently appointed to French public hospitals, have the title of hospital practitioner. There are also practitioners in part-time hospitals, who are also appointed on a permanent basis, as well as practitioners appointed on a temporary basis under different names." @en
